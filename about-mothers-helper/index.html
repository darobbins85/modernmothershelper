Our Story

I’m Katherine, a busy mom of three adventurous boys! Motherhood is an incredible journey, but let’s be honest—it’s also tough, especially when you feel like you’re navigating it without a village. After years of juggling the endless responsibilities of parenting, managing a household, and working a full-time job, I realized I wasn’t alone in feeling overwhelmed.

That’s why I created Modern Mother’s Helper. It started with a simple idea: to be the helping hand that all moms deserve but few actually have. I wanted to create a service that provides moms and families with real support, the kind that lightens the load and brings more joy into daily life.

At Modern Mother’s Helper, we understand the mental load of motherhood because we’ve been there. That’s why our services are designed to help you reclaim your time and energy. Whether it’s organizing your home, running errands, taking down holiday decorations, or simply helping you reset your space, we’re here to make life easier and more manageable.
