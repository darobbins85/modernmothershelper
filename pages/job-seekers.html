Interested In Joining Modern Mother's Helper?

Are you organized, caring, and ready to make a real difference in the lives of busy families? Join Modern Mother's Helper in Austin, TX, where we’re redefining support for moms by giving them the help they deserve!

About Us:
We are on a mission to support moms and families by providing the extra help they need to navigate their busy days. From tidying up, to organizing a messy pantry, to tackling that mountain of laundry piling up, or picking up and unloading groceries, we make life easier and more manageable for parents juggling it all.

What You'll Be Doing:

  • Light Housekeeping: Tidy up, organize, and assist with everyday household tasks.
  • Meal Prep: Help with simple, family-friendly meals or snack prep.
  • Organizing: Sorting, decluttering, and creating systems that simplify access and improve usability.
  • Errands: Lend a hand with groceries, pickups, or other small tasks that lighten the load.
  • Anything/Everything: Our goal is to be as helpful as possible, so any tasks that are needed, we say yes!
 

Why You'll Love This Job:

  • Flexible hours that work with your schedule.
  • A fulfilling role where you’ll directly impact the lives of busy moms.
  • Competitive pay.
  • Creative Problem Solving: Every home and family is unique, offering you the chance to use your creativity to find customized solutions that work for each client.
  • Personal Growth: This job offers the chance to develop new skills in organization, time management, and customer service while making a real difference in people’s lives

We’re Looking For Someone Who:

  • Has experience with housekeeping, children, or both.
  • Is reliable, punctual, and communicative.
  • Brings a positive attitude and is a natural problem-solver.
  • This position is great for: nanny's, teachers, stay at home moms, servers/bartenders, college students, and anybody that gets joy from helping people and knowing that the work you're doing directly impacts a mom in need of support.
 

Position Requirements:

  • Must be at least 18 years of age.
  • Must be willing to submit to background check.
  • Emotional Intelligence and a willingness to anticipate needs and ask for guidance when necessary.
  • Must be able to follow instructions and ensure that all tasks are completed the way the client prefers.  
  • Time Management - Must be extremely efficient to ensure as many tasks as possible can be completed.
  • Must have reliable transportation.
  • Must be able to lift 30-40 lbs and stand/walk for extended periods of time.
 

Join Us and Make a Difference!
If you’re looking for a flexible, meaningful role where you can bring your skills to help families thrive, we’d love to hear from you! Apply today and be part of a team that’s changing the game for moms everywhere.
