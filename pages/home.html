Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Mother's Helper?

A Mother’s Helper is someone who provides household support to make daily life easier for families. Some call us “personal assistants” or “family assistants” but we like to think of ourselves as a second “you”. Our Helpers focus on tasks like tidying up, organizing, meal prep, errands, and any other services that will help you reclaim your time.

Can I customize the tasks my helper performs?

Yes! You can create a personalized list of tasks for your Helper to complete, and this can be updated as your needs change.

Are your helpers trained?

Yes. All of our Helpers are W-2 employees that are thoroughly vetted, reliable, and experienced in household management. They undergo extensive background checks, reference checks, and training to ensure high-quality service.

Do You Offer Childcare?

Not at this time. Our focus is on helping with all the other tasks that make daily life easier, so you can have more time for what matters most. However, we may explore childcare services in the future.

Are there any fees or requirements for booking?

No! Our services are offered on a weekly basis so that you can use it when you need it. Our goal is to keep our services as accessible and straightforward as possible, so you only pay for the assistance you receive.

How do I schedule service?

You can book services by filling out our online form or giving us a call or email. We’ll match you with a Helper based on your schedule and needs.

What makes your company different?

We are proudly owned and operated by women! We’ve been there—the endless laundry, the cluttered countertops, the never-ending list of things to do. Most of our Helpers are moms too, so we know firsthand what it takes to keep a household running smoothly, and we’ve turned that experience into a service designed to make your life easier. We don’t just get it—we live it.

What Hours are Available For Service?

We have Helpers available to assist you 7 days a week between the hours of 7:00AM and 11:00PM.
